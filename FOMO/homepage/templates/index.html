The FOMO Experience


We started FOMO way back in 1952. Times have changed. Tastes in Music have changed. But here at FOMO our passion of aiding musicians of all ages has not changed. We are determined to help every customer find their sound. Whether that sound be jazz, rock or classical; guitar, trumpet or didgeridoo. We understand the power of music, and we want to bring that power into the homes of Utah County. FOMO is a store that aims to satisfy the musical needs of musicians and budding musicians in the area. We provide instruments, accessories, repairs, sheet music, and training opportunities. Our friendly staff is waiting to help you find the instrument perfect for you. We have the widest variety of instruments in the state and we are positive that we can find the perfect instrument for you and your family. We believe music can change the world, because music changes people. We invite you to come change the world with us.
